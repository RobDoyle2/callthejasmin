Nuloha2 VV was set up in 2002 to help companies migrate their Microsoft Windows client and server Operating Systems.

Since then we have helped companies in Ireland, the U.K., Belgium, Germany and Switzerland. Our main clients were in the fintech sector ranging from UBS AG in Zurich to Bank of Ireland and AIG Insurance in Ireland. We were an integral part of the largest Digital Transformation in Europe while working for the Department of Welfare and Pensions in the U.K.

In 2021 we partnered up with ABC Motor Engineers to help them provide Custom Declartions documentation for vehicle imports to the Irish Republic. The advent of Brexit saw an exponential growth in demand for this service and new regulations took hold.
